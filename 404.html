Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gas Tracker really free?
Yes, 100% free and open source under the MIT license. No premium tiers, no paywalls, no "freemium" features. We built this because we needed it ourselves.
Do you collect any data?
No. Gas Tracker runs entirely locally on your machine. It does not send any data to our servers or any third party. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking.
How does clipboard protection work?
The app monitors your clipboard for cryptocurrency addresses and can alert you or auto-correct if it detects a potentially malicious address swap. This prevents common "address poisoning" attacks.
Which browsers does the extension support?
The extension works on all Chromium-based browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera, and Vivaldi. Firefox support is coming soon.
Do I need Python installed?
For the desktop app, yes — Python 3.8 or newer is required. The browser extension has no Python dependency and works independently.
